Understanding Blockchain Expansion

Blockchain expansion refers to the growth and scaling of blockchain networks to accommodate more users, transactions, and applications. Stellar achieves this through:

  • Efficient consensus mechanism (Stellar Consensus Protocol)
  • Anchor system for asset issuance
  • Decentralized exchange built into the protocol
  • Interoperability with traditional banking systems

Understanding these concepts helps users make informed decisions when participating in the Stellar ecosystem.
